Task Instructions

Great job practicing. The real experimental trials start now!

Specific Instructions:

As you are aware, for this experiment we will give you a NOUN, and just below this noun, you will type out CREATIVE WORDS that are linked to the noun as they come to your mind.

By creative, we mean words that are linked to the noun, but very few, if any, other people would come up with. So, that if someone else saw the response, they would find it original and/or surprising, but still understand the connection.

It is very important that you only type SINGLE WORDS, that is, DO NOT type sentences or phrases.

Each word will appear on the screen for 2 minutes. Use the entire time period to type the creative words you think of. Again, type each CREATIVE word as soon as it comes to your mind.

The words can be any kind of word (verb, adjective, noun, adverb, etc.) but they cannot be a proper noun, so not a word that gets capitalized (e.g., Paul, Atlanta, Cheerios). Also, do not make up words, these should be real words.
